About Me

Learn More About Me

Hello! My name is Client Client Jr. I am a digital Illustrator, mostly focused on album cover art. I've worked with digital illustrations for 15 years, and I was a traditional Illustrator before that. I started drawing when I was a little kid, because I always liked to watch cartoons and I wanted to make my own. In my teenage years, I got really into music so I started doing fake album covers for fun. In my trajectory, I've worked with big and small musicians and bands, I've made a few book covers and also work with fanart commissions. I do not have a single style, so I'm able to do all sorts of covers, for different genres of music. I am currently based in Brazil, with plans to move to Ireland. I'm also able to create tattoo designs.

What Do I Do?

  • Photo editing using advanced software.
  • Professional Photoshop retouching.
  • Custom digital illustrations.
  • Album cover art design.
  • Book cover design.
  • Tattoo design.
  • Fanart commissions.

As Well As

When I'm not editing photos, I enjoy creating personal artwork and exploring new creative ideas. I'm passionate about pushing the boundaries of digital illustration and constantly learning new techniques to improve my craft.
